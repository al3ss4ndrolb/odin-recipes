Zucchini Cous Cous Amazing Easy Recipe

To make a zucchini cous cous dish you'll need a few things: Warning this is very easy! Here are the ingredients:

  • zucchini
  • cous cous
  • curry
  • olive oil
  • salt

Here are the steps:

  1. Get water to boil
  2. Pour water onto pre weighed cous cous
  3. Put pre sauteed zucchini
  4. Season to taste with salt and curry

This is all for today!!
